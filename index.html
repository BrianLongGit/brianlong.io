About Brian

Brian Long is a highly motivated developer with a strong background in full-stack web development, strategic planning/marketing, and technical writing.

He created BrianLong.IO with the goal to help small-business owners get more customers.

In addition to this, Brian holds a Bachelors Degree at University of California Santa Barbara in History of Public Policy where he wrote a senior thesis on improving the community in his hometown.

Before he became a developer, Brian worked as a caregiver and behavioral technician for children with autism in Orange County throughout college. Here he learned to become more compassionate with underserved communities and plans to continue to give back in the near future.

Outside of work, Brian enjoys reading books, working out, playing basketball, finding new places to eat and explore, and listening to The Joe Rogan Experience podcast.

Brian is currently looking for new opportunities as a software developer in Orange County and is always learning.
